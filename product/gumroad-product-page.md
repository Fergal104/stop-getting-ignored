## Long Description

If your outreach is being ignored, the problem is usually not effort. It is weak framing.

Most freelancers send messages that are too generic, too flattering, or too needy. This kit fixes that with practical templates built around a simple principle: show a real observation, make the value easy to see, and ask for a low-friction next step.

Stop Getting Ignored gives you working starting points for cold email and DMs, follow-up copy that does not sound desperate, proposal starters for when someone shows interest, and a basic pipeline so you stop losing leads in your own notes.

This is not a course. It is an execution pack.

## FAQ

### Is this niche-specific?

No. The templates are written to adapt across common service businesses. Use the personalization prompts to tailor them.

### Does this replace personalization?

No. It removes blank-page friction and gives you a cleaner starting structure.

### How do I use it?

Pick a message closest to your service, add one visible observation about the prospect, then use the follow-up and proposal sections if they reply.

### What format is included?

The kit is delivered as a Markdown document you can open anywhere and copy from quickly.
