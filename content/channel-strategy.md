# Channel Strategy

## Primary Channels

- X: short ideas, rewrites, and contrarian outreach takes
- Threads: slightly more conversational versions of X posts

## Posting Cadence

- X: 1 post per weekday at 09:30 Europe/London
- Threads: 1 companion post per weekday at 09:30 Europe/London
- Do not launch a newsletter until at least 25 email captures exist

## Content Mix

- 40 percent message teardowns
- 25 percent better-opener lessons
- 15 percent follow-up advice
- 10 percent CTA and proposal advice
- 10 percent product or lead-magnet mentions

## CTA Rules

- Use the free pack CTA in every third post at most
- Mention the paid kit directly only after a value-heavy post or in email
- Use the landing page as the primary bio link on both platforms

## Account Setup Defaults

- Pin one post that explains the problem: generic outreach gets ignored
- Put the landing-page link in the bio
- Use the same bio language across platforms for consistency
- Keep the profile faceless and practical; no fake founder backstory

## First 7 Days

- Day 1: publish posts 1 and 2
- Day 2: publish posts 3 and 4
- Day 3: publish post 5 and a Threads variant
- Day 4: publish posts 6 and 7
- Day 5: publish post 8 and a Threads variant
- Day 6: publish posts 9 and 10
- Day 7: review top hooks and repeat the strongest angle with a new example
